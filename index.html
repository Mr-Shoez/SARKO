The Blueprint

Restoring the Kingdom

01.

Who We Are

Led by Her Royal Highness Princess Dipuo Mokola Mthembu Toto Seboko of the Batlharo Kingdom, SARKO is a political entity integrating traditional monarchical authority with modern governance. We are the shield against the erosion of indigenous rights.

02.

Our Mission

"Preserving the wealth and welfare of the Nations." We reject the concept of a single "rainbow nation." Instead, we view South Africa as a collection of distinct indigenous Nations, each requiring protection of their language, culture, and territorial rights.

03.

International Sovereignty

SARKO operates on a global stage. In 2024, we signed a historic bilateral agreement with the United States of KAILASA. Our leadership has been conferred the KAILASA Ratna Mani Award, asserting that traditional kingdoms hold status equal to modern nation-states.

04.

Proven Governance

We do not just speak; we govern. Following the 2021 elections, SARKO secured seats in the Joe Morolong and Maluti-A-Phofung Local Municipalities, forming strategic coalitions to enforce clean audits and service delivery.

The Foundation of Dignity

Agriculture & Land

The time has arrived for the land to go back to its rightful owners. We advocate for land restitution directly to Royal Houses rather than the state.

Our mandate ensures communities live off their land. We demand that profits from agricultural and mining resources go directly to the local population, not external corporations.
