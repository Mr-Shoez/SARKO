The Blueprint

Restoring the Kingdom

01.

Who We Are

We are the guardians of heritage. SARKO is not merely an organization; it is a movement dedicated to the preservation of land, dignity, and the royal lineage of the African people. We stand as a bridge between ancient wisdom and modern governance.

02.

Our Mission

To reclaim what was lost. Our mission is to legally and socially restore the custodianship of the land to its rightful heirs, ensuring economic freedom and social justice through traditional leadership structures that have stood the test of time.

03.

Our Vision

A sovereign, self-sustaining society where traditional values dictate our future. We envision a world where the dignity of the African child is protected by the shield of their ancestors and the spear of economic independence.

04.

Our Story

Forged in the fires of Maluti-a-Phofung, our story began with a refusal to accept decay. From a small council of elders to a nationwide movement, we have grown through the realization that true power lies in unity and the soil beneath our feet.

Core Principles

Pillars of Dignity

Land Reclaim

Poverty alleviation through land redistribution. Our mission is to ensure that our communities live off their land and that mineral resources benefit the people directly.

Whether agricultural or mining, the wealth of the soil must return to the communities living on it. We safeguard the mineral resources of the country for the future.

Restoring the Soil

Shelter & Security

Providing security for housing, jobs, and lives. Protecting women and children through outreach and strict law enforcement.

Health Restoration

Revitalizing the collapsing system by incentivizing young professionals and introducing health careers at a school level.

Education & Skills

Skills workshops in gardening, sewing, and carpentry. Promoting spiritual values while upgrading the system for free education.
