03. Target Personas

The Disenfranchised Traditionalist

Profile: Male/Female, 45+, rural.

Motivation: Feels culture is sold out. Wants return to order and respect.

The Land Claimant

Profile: Any age.

Motivation: Frustrated by slow land reform. Wants aggressive action.

The Vulnerable Mother

Profile: Female, 25-50.

Motivation: Terrified of GBV. Drawn to strict enforcement for protection.

The Unemployed Youth

Profile: Male, 18-35 (Secondary).

Motivation: Looking for tangible skills training (carpentry, sewing).

04. Execution Guidelines

DO

Highlight specific successes in Maluti-a-Phofung as proof of capability.

Use titles of traditional leaders (King, Queen, Inkosi) with strict protocol.

DON'T

Use "WhatsApp quality" images. Use stylized graphics or B&W if hi-res is unavailable.

Emphasize "Kailasa" connection on the homepage. Keep international partners vague unless benefits are tangible.
